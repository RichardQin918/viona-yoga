VIONA YOGA

DIVE INTO YOURSELF - A REAL SANCTUARY

Yoga is more than a physical practice. VIONA YOGA offers holistic yoga practices, which cover a wide span of methodologies that enable you to explore yourself in multiple dimensions. Ultimately, it is an inner self-discovery and self-awareness cultivation journey.

Viona’s classes concentrate on practical guidance, simplify and minimize words so that there is enough space for your inner world. When it comes to mindfulness, it is not about knowing, it is about doing.
