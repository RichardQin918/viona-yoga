Yoga with Viona

When was the last time you felt completely relaxed?

"Yoga is for everyone. It does not matter whether or not you're flexible. It is not about touching your toes with your fingers. The main purpose of Yoga is to come back to your body from your busy headspace. Through uniting our body and mind, we will gradually increase self-awareness, and release the tension and stress that we have piled up throughout our daily lives. Yoga leads us to find inner peace" - Viona

Practice with me

My mission is to introduce more people to the benefits of real yoga. We live in an over-commercialized world - the concept of yoga has been adapted to the modern society and lost its authenticity. My yoga classes focus on guiding students to connect with their body, to shift their busy mind to be in the present moment, and to embrace themselves. May your journey with yoga lead you to a path to meet a happier you.
